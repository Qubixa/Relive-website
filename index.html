About Us

Relive Dance and Fitness

Founded by Ashwini Soni, a passionate dancer and fitness enthusiast, Relive Dance and Fitness has been a beacon of creativity and health in Mumbai. After a successful and fruitful corporate run for over 4 years, she decided to share her love for dance and fitness with the world. She envisioned a space where people could groove to their favorite beats while improving their overall well-being.

The Journey: From humble beginnings, Relive Dance and Fitness grew into a vibrant community. Classes ranging from bollywood freestyle to semi classical Indian dance to other variety of styles became popular. Ashwini's dedication and expertise attracted dancers of all ages and backgrounds.

Impact: Relive Dance and Fitness isn't just about dance; it's a lifestyle. Students not only learn dance choreographies but also build their strength, flexibility, and confidence. The studio hosts workshops, performances, and wellness events, fostering a sense of belonging.

Vision

As Relive Dance and Fitness continues to evolve, Ashwini remains committed to spreading joy through movement. Whether you're pirouetting or doing squats, this studio is where dreams come alive.
