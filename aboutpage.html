About Relive Dance Studio

Where movement meets memory

Our Journey

The Relive Story

Founded by Ashwini Soni, a passionate dancer and fitness enthusiast, Relive Dance and Fitness has been a beacon of creativity and health in Mumbai. After a successful corporate career spanning over 4 years, she decided to share her love for dance and fitness with the world.

What began as a small dance class has blossomed into a vibrant community where students of all ages discover the joy of movement. Our studio offers a unique blend of traditional and contemporary dance styles, creating a space where artistry meets athleticism.

Our Vision

At Relive Dance and Fitness, we believe dance is more than just movement - it's a way to express, connect, and transform. Our vision is to create a space where every individual, regardless of age or experience, can discover the joy of dance while building strength, flexibility, and confidence.

We're committed to fostering a community that celebrates diversity, creativity, and personal growth through the art of movement.

Meet The Founder

Ashwini Soni

From Corporate to Choreography

After a successful 4-year corporate career, Ashwini followed her passion for dance and founded Relive Dance & Fitness. Her unique journey brings a professional approach to dance instruction while maintaining the creativity and joy that dance embodies.

Dance is the hidden language of the soul. At Relive, we don't just teach steps - we help people discover their own rhythm and express their true selves through movement.
